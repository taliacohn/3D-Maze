Rules

Help Mr.Robot find his friend in the maze!
Choose the size of the maze by filling in the columns, rows, and levels above. Make sure to check all the floors!

Left arrow on keyboard (←): move left in maze

Right arrow on keyboard (→): move right in maze

Up arrow on keyboard (↑): move upwards in maze

Down arrow on keyboard (↓): move down in maze

W key (w): move up to next level

S key (s): move down to level below
